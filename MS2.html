Information Technology

Information technology (IT) is the use of any computers, storage, networking and other physical devices, infrastructure and processes to create, process, store, secure and exchange all forms of electronic data.

Importance of IT

Information technology helps to build and grow the commerce and business sector and generate maximum possible output.
The time taken by different sectors to generate business is now minimised with an advancement in Information technology.
It provides electronic security, storage, and efficient communication.
